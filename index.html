Background

My name is James and I live in Hamilton Ontario, Canada. I've always had an interest in tech and decided to make a shift to the world of web development in 2020. I made the decision in 2021 to join the online teaching company Treehouse and enroll in their front end tech degree program. The program has been incredibly benfefical and has helped me craft practical skills through a project centric curriculum. Currently I am doing some contract work while I finish my final unit in the tech degree program.
